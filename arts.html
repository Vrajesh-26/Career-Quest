ARTS & DESIGN CAREERS

Graphic Designer

Graphic Designers create visual concepts to communicate ideas. ...

Job Description: Develop layouts, logos, illustrations.

Required Skills: Adobe Creative Suite, typography, color theory.

Educational Background: Bachelor's in Graphic Design.

Average Salary: ₹3-12 LPA

Top Employers: Advertising agencies, Design studios.

Experience Required: Portfolio most important.

Interior Designer

Interior Designers make spaces functional and beautiful. ...

Job Description: Plan spaces, select materials.

Required Skills: Spatial awareness, CAD software.

Educational Background: Bachelor's in Interior Design.

Average Salary: ₹4-15 LPA

Top Employers: Design firms, Architecture firms.

Experience Required: 1-3 years.
