LAW & PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION CAREERS

Lawyer

Lawyers advise and represent clients on legal issues and disputes. ...

Job Description: Research laws, argue cases, draft legal documents.

Required Skills: Analytical skills, research, public speaking.

Educational Background: LLB degree (3 or 5 year program).

Average Salary: ₹5-30 LPA

Top Employers: Law firms, Corporations, Government.

Experience Required: Internship during law school.

Civil Services (IAS/IPS)

Civil servants implement government policies and administer public services. ...

Job Description: Policy implementation, public administration.

Required Skills: Leadership, decision-making, problem-solving.

Educational Background: Any bachelor's degree.

Average Salary: ₹8-25 LPA (plus benefits)

Top Employers: Central/State governments.

Experience Required: Must clear UPSC examination.
