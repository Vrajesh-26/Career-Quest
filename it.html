Artificial Intelligence (AI) Engineer

An AI Engineer designs, develops, and deploys AI-powered systems, such as machine learning models, deep learning applications, and intelligent automation tools.
Their role involves training AI models to analyze patterns, make decisions, and automate complex tasks across industries like healthcare, finance, and robotics. ...

Job Description: AI Engineers develop intelligent algorithms and models for automation, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. They build and deploy AI-powered applications for various industries, such as healthcare, finance, and manufacturing.
Required Skills: Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, machine learning, deep learning, data structures, mathematics.
Educational Background: B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science, AI & ML, Data Science, or related fields.
Average Salary: ₹10-25 LPA
Top Companies: Google, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Microsoft, NVIDIA
Experience Required: 0-2 years (entry-level); 3-5 years for mid-level AI projects.
