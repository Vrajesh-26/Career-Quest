MEDICAL & HEALTHCARE CAREERS

Doctor (General Physician)

General Physicians diagnose and treat illnesses and health conditions. ...

Job Description: Examine patients, order tests, make diagnoses.

Required Skills: Medical knowledge, diagnostic skills, patient care.

Educational Background: MBBS degree + MD specialization.

Average Salary: ₹8-30 LPA

Top Employers: Hospitals, Clinics, Healthcare centers.

Experience Required: 5+ years including residency.

Surgeon

Surgeons perform operations to treat diseases and injuries. ...

Job Description: Perform surgical procedures, monitor recovery.

Required Skills: Surgical skills, precision, stamina.

Educational Background: MBBS + MS in Surgery.

Average Salary: ₹15-50 LPA

Top Employers: Hospitals, Specialty clinics.

Experience Required: 5+ years post specialization.
